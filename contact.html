Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for events?

You can register for events through our events page. Simply click on the event you're interested in and follow the registration process.

Are events free for students?

Most campus events are free for HITAM students. Some special workshops or conferences may have a nominal fee, which will be clearly mentioned.

Can external participants join events?

Yes, many of our events are open to external participants. Check the event details for specific eligibility criteria and registration requirements.

How do I stay updated about new events?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow our social media channels, or regularly check our events page for the latest updates and announcements.

What if I need to cancel my registration?

You can cancel your registration by contacting our events team. Please refer to the specific event's cancellation policy for details.

Do you provide certificates for events?

Yes, we provide participation certificates for most workshops, seminars, and conferences. Details will be mentioned in the event description.
